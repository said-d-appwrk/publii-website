For new users
ExcelPipe quickly and easily searches and replaces across thousands of Excel spreadsheets. [more]

To use ExcelPipe:

Drag and drop your files into ExcelPipe's window, or use the Files to Process tab to Browse for a folder or List of Files.
On the Actions tab, type in each Find What/Replace With pair, and also where the 'Find What' should be found.
You can also specify an Excel search/replace list with Unicode text.
Using the Look In location, you can search in hyperlinks, body text, cells, OLE Links and more.
On the Options tab, check backup mode, passwords, and restart control.
Go! Click either the 'Start replacing' or 'Start searching' button at the bottom of the screen. You can generate a list of hyperlinks by clicking the Hyperlink report checkbox.
Remember - just press F1 for help at any time, or choose Index or Contents from the Help Menu.
